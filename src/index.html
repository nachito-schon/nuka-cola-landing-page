Ingredients

Nuka Cola is made with pure, filtered water, high fructose corn syrup, caramel color, phosphoric acid, natural flavors, and just a touch of radiation.

Nutritional Information

Each 12-ounce serving of Nuka Cola contains 140 calories, 35 grams of sugar, and 20 millirems of radiation.

Serving Suggestions

Nuka Cola is best enjoyed ice cold, straight from the bottle or poured over a tall glass of ice.
